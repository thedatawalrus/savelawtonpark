What's Happening?

AAPS has determined that Lawton Elementary needs to be torn down and replaced. This process is to be supported using the 2019 Bond money.

The conceptual site plan that AAPS has put forth for New Lawton places the new school on the playground and field behind the existing school, which then requires the use of the City's Lawton Park for the playground and recess areas.

However, if this plan goes through, we will lose almost half of the open green space that is currently available on the combined AAPS-City properties. We will go from a combined 7.0 acres of open greenspace to 3.8 acres.

  • The amount of area that can be dark at night for wildlife and people is decreasing by 46%.
